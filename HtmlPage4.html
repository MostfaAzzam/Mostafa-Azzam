Free online certificates

Acadmia Maz platform grants an accredited certificate for both individual courses and integrated diplomas, and all the certificates it awards are completely free once you finish studying the course or diploma online.


Steps to obtain the free certificate

  • Log in to your Acadmia Maz account
  • Join a course
  • Study course lessons online without introducing or accelerating the lesson video
  • Congratulations, the certificate will be sent to you via email and saved in your Acadmia Maz account upon completion of the course lessons.
